This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices in which Bitplay Club collect, uses, maintains collected information from users (each, a "User") of the https://bitplay.club/ website ("Site"). To provide you with our Services, we need to collect some of your personal information.

We may collect it when users register on the Site, sign in their accounts, make a payment and/or a withdrawal. We only collect information that users voluntarily provide us. Users can always refuse to provide personal information, bit it may prevent them from being able to use certain Site related functionality. We will store your personal information for as long as we need to fulfill the purposes for which we collect your information.

We may collect non-personal information about Users whenever they interact with our Site. Bitplay Club uses the collected information for the following purposes:

To improve customer service: information provided by Users let us cover all customer support requests more efficiently

To improve our Site: we may use feedback provided by Users to improve our services

To send periodic emails: we may use the email address to send information and updates regarding out services

How we protect your personal information

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information from loss, theft, disclosure, copy or unauthorized access. Sensitive and private data exchange between the Site and its Users happens over a SSL secured communication channel and is encrypted and protected with digital signatures. Your info is protected both online and offline.

How we use cookies

We use cookies that collect non-personal information from visitors of our website to track the performance of the website and improve it for the better user experience.
