1. T&C of the Referral Program

1.1 Any customer can participate in the Referral program run by Bitplay Club.

1.2 To be eligible to participate in the Referral program as Referrer the customer needs to have an activated account with Bitplay Club lottery service.

1.3 Every customer receives its unique Referral link upon account activation. This Referral link needs to be provided to other people for becoming a referral. A new player becomes a referral after registering an account through someone's referral link. All referral links are valid 1 year upon clicking on it however we use the rule "Last Cookies Wins" which means that if an individual clicked on the referral link of a referrer A, didn't register an account and then clicked on the referral link of a referrer B he's automatically considered by the system as a referral of the last referrer, in this example it's a referrer B.

1.4 Each referrer receives a reward when its referral purchase lottery tickets. The level of this reward is stated on the Referral Program page.

1.5 All referral rewards are added to the referrer's account balance once a day after the draw is closed and all tickets are counted.

1.6 When promoting Bitplay Club service all referrers must agree that the following activities are prohibited in regards to the promotion of Bitplay club:

1.6.1 Spamming, mass emailing;

1.6.2 Using difference bots that distribute referral link;

1.6.3 Using of scripts/codes/algorithms that will message/invite individuals and send them the referral link;

1.6.4 Misleading information about Bitplay Club lottery service;

1.6.5 Creating multiple accounts at Bitplay Club;

1.6.6 Creating websites, domains, social medias, email address containing Bitplay Club trade mark or imitating its beloging to Bitplay Service;

1.7 Bitplay Club customers agree not to use any prohibited content while participation in Referral Program. Prohibited content is:

1.7.1 Content which promotes racism, bigotry, hatred, discrimination or physical harm of any kind against any group or individual;

1.7.2 Offensive, abusive, intimidating or harassing content;

1.7.3 Content that is sexually explicit, obscene and/or contains nudity;

1.7.4 Any political and/or religious statement;

1.7.5 Content containing images or reference to drugs, alchol, tobacco, weapons or firearms;

1.7.6 Content that violates someone's else's privacy.

1.8 Bitplay Club customers agree not to deceive or abuse Bitplay Club or its customers; will not violate Bitplay Club Referral Program condistions.
